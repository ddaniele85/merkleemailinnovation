The Benefits

Email continues to be the most effective marketing channel, with an ROI of 36:1. That’s why it’s important to constantly improve your subscribers’ experience—and interactive email is the opportunity to do just that.

According to Martech Advisor, interactive email has the power to increase the rate of click-to-open by 73%. Subscribers want it, too.

Here’s how adding interactive features to your emails can benefit your campaigns:

High-Impact Engagement Metrics

• Up to 300% Increase in CTR:
Adding interactive elements or embedded videos can boost click-through rates by up to 300%.

• 73% Higher Click-to-Open Rate:
Interactive elements increase the efficiency of emails, with one study reporting a 73% higher click-to-open rate.

• Higher Response Rates:
Interactive surveys and polls can boost user response rates by up to 520% compared to traditional, static links.

• Higher Engagement:
Interactive emails are estimated to produce 80% higher engagement rates than static emails.

• 60% Willingness to Engage:
Roughly 60% of consumers report they are more inclined to engage with interactive email content

Conversion and Revenue Impact

• 2x More Conversions:
Interactive content generates roughly 2x more conversions than static content.

• 160% Higher Conversion Rate:
Interactive forms (e.g., filling out a form or quiz inside the email) can boost conversion rates by up to 160%.

• Double the Revenue:
Interactive campaigns consistently generate double the revenue per recipient compared to static campaigns.

• 1,430% Increase in Traffic:
Some case studies, such as Souplantation, have reported up to a 1,430% increase in traffic using interactive elements.

Marketing Advantages & User Preferences

• 91% Consumer Preference:
91% of recipients prefer interactive and visual content over static, yet less than 20% of brands are currently offering this experience.

• 88% Brand Differentiation:
88% of marketers consider interactive content an effective way to differentiate their brand from competitors.

• 47% Increase in Loyalty:
Gamified interactive emails can lead to a 47% increase in engagement and a 22% increase in customer loyalty.

• Lower Abandonment:
By reducing the need to click away to a slow landing page, interactive emails minimize user drop-off in the sales funnel.
