Accordion

Expertise Accordion

Accordions in emails are interactive, collapsible design elements that allow users to click headers to reveal more content, significantly reducing scrolling and saving space, particularly on mobile devices. They are ideal for FAQs and long-form content, often implemented using AMP for Email technology or advanced CSS3

After navigation and menus in email, it’s also very much possible to actually use accordion in emails. CSS3 Accordion uses CSS3 techniques to develop accordions that are mainly used for mobile emails.

The accordion will work with most mobile email clients as well as in Apple mail and Outlook for Mac on desktop. The accordion will work with email systems that support external CSS only. Windows based clients might not support such accordion as it uses CSS3. The accordions will also not work with gmail app and third party apps.

This email is a normal email layout for desktop user(except Apple Mail and Outlook MAC), but it gets converted to accordion when users read it on mobile email client, native apps.
